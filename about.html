ART IS NOT A CAREER, IT'S A LIFE - ANDY GOLDWORTHY.



I have always had an eye for detail and passion for design. I have studied Art and Design at every opportunity, most recently receiving a First Class Bachelor of the Arts Degree, with Honours, in Graphic Design, from Nottingham Trent University in July 2022.


My artwork has been used all over the world, from local farms in Buckinghamshire, to exhibitions in Brazil. During my design career, my work has featured on Stefan Sagmeister’s Instagram page, been displayed in an open-air exhibition in São Paulo, Brazil, been placed in the Summer Exhibition in the D13 Gallery in Doncaster and has won a Binary Botanical competition.
